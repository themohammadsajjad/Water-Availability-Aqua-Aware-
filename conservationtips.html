Water Conservation Tips

Effective tips for saving water and preserving our resources

Save Water, Save Life

💧

Turn Off the Tap

Save gallons by turning off the faucet while brushing your teeth or washing your face.

🔧

Fix Leaks

Repair leaky faucets and pipes to prevent water wastage.

🚿

Install Water-Saving Fixtures

Use water-efficient showerheads and low-flow faucets to conserve water.

🌿

Water Plants Wisely

Water plants in the early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation.

⏳

Shorten Showers

Reducing shower time saves gallons of water each day.

🌧️

Collect Rainwater

Use rain barrels to collect and store rainwater for garden use.
